A father dreamed of owning this land for over sixty years. His son Robert Acuna made that dream real - and transformed it into Alexandra's at Gwin. Just minutes from the city, this is the RGV's only true private luxury retreat. Acreage, pool, cabin, pavilion. A place to unwind, reset, and return ready.
