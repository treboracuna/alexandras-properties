Our Story

A Family.
A Vision. A Legacy.

Robert and Leticia Acuna didn't start with much. What they had was a vision, a work ethic that never quit, and a belief that the Rio Grande Valley was worth betting on. That was enough.

Twenty-five years ago, Robert pointed at an empty lot near where they raised their family and made a promise - he would build something in his daughter's name. Jennifer Alexandra. The only daughter. The middle child. The heart of everything this family does.

That lot was taken before the dream could be planted. A funeral home rose where the vision once stood. But the vision never died - because the man who held it never let it go.

Alexandra's Plaza is now under construction in Edinburg, Texas. Twenty-five years in the making. This is not a real estate company. This is an American story still being written.
