Our Story

A Family.
A Vision.
A Legacy.

Alexandra's Properties was not born in a boardroom. It was born from a father standing on an empty lot near the family's childhood home — pointing at the ground and saying, one day, we build here. Twenty years of that vision. Now it's concrete and steel.

"We don't develop properties. We engineer the places that define what the Rio Grande Valley becomes."

Three men. Two generations. A father whose decades in insurance and financial services taught him what lasting institutions look like. His brother who turns blueprints into standing structures. And his youngest son Trebor — driving financial strategy and scaling every project with precision.

The company carries the name of Jennifer Alexandra Perez — the only daughter, the middle child, her father's right hand across every venture he has ever built. Not a token. A pillar.
